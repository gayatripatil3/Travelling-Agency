A travel agency is a company or organization that assists individuals or groups in planning, organizing, and booking travel-related arrangements. Travel agencies act as intermediaries between travelers and various travel service providers, such as airlines, hotels, car rental companies, cruise lines, and tour operators.The primary purpose of a travel agency is to provide expertise, guidance, and personalized services to travelers. They help clients in choosing destinations, making travel arrangements, and arranging necessary accommodations and transportation. Travel agencies also provide information about travel requirements, such as visas, passports, and health regulations, and can assist in obtaining the necessary documentation.b>
